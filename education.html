Lambda School

Full Stack Web Development Course.

Lambdas online program uses and teaches the current trends of web development along with teaching the core computer science model. Unlike traditional college, Lambda is up to date with the current web development trends and offer real world experiences programming along side of our piers. At Lambda we engage in real projects and actively use the technologies that is current.

Front End

Our front end development training went for 10 solid weeks. Each week consisting of a sprint. Each week ending with a sprint challenge. Through persistance we would demonstrate to our project managers that we not only understood the circulme but were also able to use and deploy the techniques that were given to us. During our first 5 week we covered web foundations such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, including the new ES6 syntax, GIT, and UI. Then for the next 4 weeks we went over React, Redux, React Router, Ajax, and functional programming techniques.

Back End

From weeks 11 through 15 we covered Node.js, Express, SQL, SQLite, Authentication and OAuth. Creating databases, storing, fetching, reading, and deleting data. Api development, and best practices for serving information to applications and to web sites.

Computer Science

Our 4 week computer science education consisted of Intro to Python/OOP, algorithms, data structures, graphs, intro to Django, intro to c, theory of commutation/Has Tables, computer architecture, and web server. All while continuing to use and hone our front end and backend skills. We had project weeks where we would work on projects for a longer duration giving us the experience of working in a team atmosphere and further integrating the use of our skills in a real world setting.

Lambda Labs

Lambda labs is a immersive capstone experience where we are all split into small teams and assigned a project to work on. Each team starts the week with a board full of tasks to complete, wireframes, and design documents. We started each day with a stand up meeting led by our PM, then we are assigned taskes and objects to complete. We participate in code reviews and pair programming.

Place kept open for capstone project placement.
